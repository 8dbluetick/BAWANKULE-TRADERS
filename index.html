How We Work

Our Project Execution Path

We follow a transparent, professional, step-by-step workflow to ensure complete alignment and top-tier project results.

01

Requirement Discussion

We discuss your architectural drawings, design ideas, load requirements, and material preferences to plan the project constraints.

02

Site Inspection

Our structural team visits your location in Lakhni or surrounding areas to measure physical dimensions and assess foundation load capability.

03

Design & Planning

We prepare precise CAD layouts, calculate stress tolerances, budget material requirements, and share structural quotes for approval.

04

Fabrication

Our skilled welders cut, bend, shape, weld, and apply anti-rust zinc-chromate primer coating inside our controlled high-capacity workshop.

05

Installation

We transport completed modules to your site and perform heavy-duty bolting, anchoring, and final on-site welding for stability.

06

Project Handover

We conduct final alignment tests, complete touch-up painting, remove debris, inspect weld integrity, and handover the finished structure.
